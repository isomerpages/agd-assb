Appointment and Re-appointment of Members to Advisory Committee
===============================================================

The Minister for Finance has re-appointed the following members for a two-year term from 1 January 2026 to 31 December 2027:

•	Associate Professor Lee Kin Wai (Associate Professor, Nanyang Technological University);

•	Ms Cindy Sim Poh Leng (Senior Director (Finance, Procurement & Admin), Maritime and Port Authority of Singapore); and 

•	Mr Michael Sim (Executive Director, Platanetree Capital Pte Ltd). 

In addition, the following new members have been appointed for a two-year term from 1 January 2026 to 31 December 2027:

•	Ms Lim Hwei Chen (Ex-officio - Divisional Director (Surveillance & Standards Division), Accounting and Corporate Regulatory Authority);

•	Mr Maurice Loh (Partner, PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP); 

•	Ms Ng Lip Nin (Ex-officio main member – Group Director, Auditor-General’s Office) / Ms Candy Hong Keat Wee (Ex-officio alternate member – Audit Director, Auditor-General’s Office); and

•	Dr Holly Yang (Associate Professor, Singapore Management University). 

The Secretariat would like to thank the following members, who have stepped down from the Advisory Committee with effect from 1 January 2026, for their services during their term:

•	Ms Chan Yen San (Partner, KPMG LLP);

•	Ms Khong Beng Yen (Ex-officio main member - Group Director, Auditor-General’s Office) / Ms Tan Soh Hian (Ex-officio alternate member - Audit Director, Auditor-General’s Office) ; 

•	Assoc Prof Andrew Lee (Associate Professor, Singapore Management University); 

•	Ms Tan Wee Khim (Ex-officio- Technical Director, Accounting and Corporate Regulatory Authority); and

•	Mrs Tay-Tok Seok Ling (Chief Financial Officer, Ngee Ann Polytechnic).
